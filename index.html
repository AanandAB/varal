// Our Story

The Pride of Kerala's Waters

The Snakehead — known in Kerala as Varal (വരാൽ) — is one of the most sought-after freshwater fish in South India. Prized for its firm, white flesh, minimal bones, and rich medicinal value.

We raise our Varal in clean, controlled freshwater farms across central Kerala. Sustainable aquaculture — no chemicals, consistent quality, peak flavour every time.

The Heritage

The Pride of
Kerala's Waters

The Snakehead — traditionally known as Varal (വരാൽ) — is the most undisputed king of freshwater fish in South India. It is highly prized for its firm, bone-free white flesh and immense medicinal properties.

We breed and raise our Varal (measuring 10 to 25 cm) in pristine, highly oxygenated freshwater ponds using strictly organic feed. No antibiotics. No chemical treatments. Just the purest taste of Kerala.

"വരാൽ ഉണ്ടെങ്കിൽ വിരുന്ന് ഒരുക്കാം" When there is Varal, the royal feast is ready.
